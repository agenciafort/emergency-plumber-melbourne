What emergency plumbing services do you offer in Melbourne?

We offer 24/7 blocked drain clearing, burst and leaking pipe repair, hot water system repair and replacement, gas leak detection and gas fitting, blocked toilet repair, and emergency roof and stormwater plumbing across metropolitan Melbourne.

How fast can an emergency plumber reach me?

Typically 45–60 minutes from your call, depending on suburb and time of day. Inner-city and south Melbourne suburbs often see faster response. We confirm ETA when you call [TELEFONE].

Are your plumbers licensed in Victoria?

Yes. All plumbing and gas work is performed by VBA-licensed professionals. Verify any licence at vba.vic.gov.au. Unlicensed work voids home insurance — never hire an unlicensed plumber for emergency repairs.

Do you provide upfront pricing?

Yes. We quote a fixed price before starting work. After-hours premiums may apply for genuine emergencies outside business hours, but you always approve the cost first. See our cost guide for typical ranges.

Can you help with insurance claims after water damage?

Yes. We provide written plumber's reports, photos and itemised invoices suitable for home insurance claims. Read our burst pipe insurance guide for documentation tips.
